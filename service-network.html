Network Infrastructure

Networks engineered to protect, scale, and never go dark.

From the campus edge to multi-site WAN and the data center fabric, NetGenX designs, segments, and operates software-defined networks built for performance, deep visibility, and zero-trust by default. No flat networks. No blind spots. No 2 a.m. outages.

The challenge

Most enterprise networks were built for a threat model that no longer exists.

Decades of bolt-on growth leave teams operating networks they can't fully see, segment, or trust — and every gap is an opening for an attacker or an outage.

Flat networks, no segmentation

A single VLAN sprawl means one compromised laptop can reach the domain controller, the ERP, and the OT floor. Without micro-segmentation, lateral movement is unrestricted and a minor breach becomes an enterprise-wide incident.

Poor visibility & telemetry

Teams run on stale spreadsheets and SNMP polling that miss east-west traffic entirely. When you can't see what's talking to what, you can't troubleshoot in minutes, prove compliance, or detect the quiet traffic that precedes a breach.

Brittle WAN across sites

Branches backhaul SaaS and voice over a single MPLS circuit with no failover. One carrier blip drops a site, jitter wrecks Teams and VoIP, and SaaS performance is at the mercy of the nearest hairpin instead of the nearest cloud on-ramp.

How we do it

A disciplined path from discovery to a network you can trust.

We don't rip and replace on day one. We map reality, design for zero trust, migrate without downtime, and stay on to operate it with you.

Discover & baseline

We auto-discover every device, link, and flow — building an accurate topology, dependency map, and traffic baseline. You get a clear picture of east-west traffic, shadow IT, and the riskiest paths, scored against zero-trust principles.

Architect for zero trust

We design a segmented, software-defined target state — VRFs and micro-segmentation, redundant cores, SD-WAN with direct cloud on-ramps, and policy that follows identity. Every design is documented, peer-reviewed, and mapped to your compliance obligations.

Migrate without downtime

Change is staged, tested in a lab, and rolled out in maintenance windows with validated back-out plans. We cut over site by site, validate with synthetic transactions, and keep the business running — no big-bang risk, no surprises for users.

Operate, monitor & optimize

Post-cutover, our team watches the fabric 24/7 with full-flow telemetry, capacity forecasting, and quarterly optimization reviews. Firmware, config drift, and lifecycle are managed for you — so the network keeps getting better, not worse.
