Solutions

Cross-cutting programs that tie the layers together

When the practices combine, they become outcomes the board cares about — fewer incidents, faster recovery, and audits that stop being fire drills.

Managed Security (MSSP)

End-to-end monitoring, detection, and response delivered from our Canadian-staffed SOC. We run your SIEM, EDR, and threat intel as an extension of your team — 24/7/365, measured against dwell time and recovery, not licenses sold.

Zero Trust Architecture

An identity-first model that assumes breach and verifies every user, device, and workload. We design and roll out least-privilege access, micro-segmentation, and continuous verification mapped to the NIST 800-207 framework.

Compliance & Audit

Continuous evidence collection for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PIPEDA — plus HIPAA and PCI-DSS readiness. We map controls, close gaps, and stand beside you through the auditor's questions so certification lands on the first cycle.

How we engage

A proven path from first call to fully operated

No drawn-out discovery theatre. A structured engagement that delivers a prioritized plan within 24 hours and measurable outcomes within the first quarter.

Assess

We map your attack surface, identity sprawl, and network topology — benchmarking your current posture against NIST CSF and CIS Controls to surface the gaps that matter most.

Architect

Our engineers design a zero-trust target state and a phased roadmap with clear owners, milestones, and success metrics your leadership can sign off on.

Deploy

We implement with minimal disruption — segmenting networks, vaulting credentials, hardening identities, and integrating tooling, validated against acceptance criteria at every stage.

Operate

Our 24/7 SOC takes the watch, tuning detections, hunting threats, and reporting against SLAs — so security stays a living posture, not a one-time project.
