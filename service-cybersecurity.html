Cybersecurity

Stop breaches before they spread.

A Canadian-staffed Security Operations Center watching your environment every minute of every day — pairing EDR, XDR, and threat hunting with response playbooks that contain an intrusion in minutes, not weeks.

The challenge

The threat landscape moved faster than your defenses.

Attackers now operate as funded businesses. Most mid-to-large enterprises are running yesterday's tooling against today's adversaries — and the gaps are exactly where breaches begin.

Ransomware is relentless

Ransomware-as-a-service has driven attack volume up sharply, with average recovery costs now in the millions. A single unpatched endpoint or reused credential can encrypt an entire estate overnight.

Alert fatigue, no coverage

Lean security teams drown in thousands of daily alerts with no 24/7 eyes-on-glass. Critical signals get buried in noise, and the hours between 6pm and 8am are exactly when adversaries strike.

Detection is too slow

Industry dwell time — the gap between intrusion and discovery — still stretches into days or weeks. Every hour an attacker goes unseen is an hour to escalate privileges, move laterally, and exfiltrate data.

How we do it

A managed program, not a product you babysit.

NetGenX runs as an extension of your team across a proven four-phase engagement — measured against dwell time, mean-time-to-respond, and audits passed.

Assess & baseline

We map your attack surface, inventory assets and identities, and benchmark your posture against MITRE ATT&CK and CIS Controls — delivering a prioritized findings report within 24 hours.

Deploy & instrument

We roll out EDR/XDR sensors, ship logs into a managed SIEM, and tune detections to your environment — establishing high-fidelity telemetry across endpoints, network, identity, and cloud.

Monitor & hunt 24/7

Our Canadian SOC watches your environment around the clock, triaging alerts and proactively threat-hunting for the behaviours signature tools miss — so nothing dwells unseen.

Respond & harden

When something is real, automated playbooks isolate the host and our responders contain it in minutes. After every incident we close the gap and feed lessons back into your defenses.

Best-of-breed platforms, operated by experts.

We are partnered and certified across the leading detection, response, and analytics platforms — so you get elite tooling without the licensing maze or staffing burden.
