About NetGenX

The team that runs security like it's our own enterprise.

For over fifteen years, NetGenX has stood between Canadian and global enterprises and the adversaries trying to breach them. We are network engineers, threat hunters, identity architects, and incident responders who treat your infrastructure as if our own business depended on it — because for our 500+ clients, it does.

Our mission

We exist to make a breach the least likely day in the life of every enterprise we protect.

NetGenX was founded on a simple conviction: enterprise security shouldn't depend on the size of your internal team or the luck of catching an alert at 3 a.m. By unifying Network Infrastructure, Cybersecurity, PAM, IDAM, Service Desk, and Cloud under one accountable team — backed by a 24/7 Canadian-staffed SOC — we give mid-to-large organizations the posture of a Fortune 100 security program without the headcount or the guesswork.

Our story

Fifteen years of earning the watch.

From a two-person network consultancy in Toronto to a multi-pillar managed security partner trusted across Canada and beyond — here's how we got here.

2009

Founded in Toronto

NetGenX opened its doors as a specialized network infrastructure consultancy, designing resilient Cisco-based architectures for Ontario's mid-market financial and healthcare firms.

2013

First 24/7 Security Operations Center

We stood up our own Canadian-staffed SOC, moving from project delivery to continuous threat monitoring and incident response for our earliest managed-security clients.

2016

ISO 27001 & SOC 2 Type II certified

NetGenX formalized its own information-security management system — proving to enterprise buyers that the team guarding their data holds itself to the same standards it enforces.

2019

Identity & privileged access practice launched

We expanded into PAM and IDAM with dedicated practices built on CyberArk, BeyondTrust, Okta, and Microsoft Entra ID — closing the credential-abuse gap that drives most modern breaches.

2022

500th enterprise client secured

NetGenX crossed 500 active enterprise clients and opened a second SOC for follow-the-sun coverage, extending our reach to global operations across three continents.

2026

Unified six-pillar platform

Network, Cybersecurity, PAM, IDAM, Service Desk, and Cloud now operate as one integrated practice — a single accountable team delivering 99.9% SLA uptime and measurable risk reduction.

What we stand for

Four principles that guide every engagement.

They aren't posters on a wall. They're the reasons our clients renew, expand, and refer us.

Security-first

We assume breach and design for it. Every recommendation we make is weighed against one question: does it measurably shrink your attack surface?

Client-first

We operate as an extension of your team, not a vendor at arm's length. Your outcomes — audits passed, downtime avoided — are how we keep score.

Innovation

Adversaries evolve weekly, so we do too. We continuously test new detections, zero-trust patterns, and automation so your defenses never go stale.

Reliability

99.9% SLA uptime and 24/7 coverage aren't aspirations — they're commitments we report on transparently, every month, against agreed targets.
